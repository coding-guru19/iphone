About EduAware SA

Our mission is simple: clear info, quick help, real support.

We’re a small South African team committed to making it easier to learn, report, and get assistance on Education, Bullying, GBVF, Teen Pregnancy, Substance Abuse, and HIV.

Mission

Make help easy to find

We organise reliable information, verified helplines, and practical steps so families and learners can act fast when it matters.

Vision

Stronger, safer communities

Every school and household should have simple tools to learn, report, and get support — online or in person.

Approach

Local, accessible, human

Plain language, SA-focused links, and real people behind the screen. We keep content short, accurate, and action-oriented.

Our story

EduAware SA started as a simple idea: put trustworthy guidance in one place and make it easy to contact people who care. From a small pilot site, we’re growing with partners, schools, and volunteers.

  • 2024: Pilot launched with core topics and a help-modal.
  • 2025: Expanding verified helplines, clinic maps, and reporting pathways.
  • Next: Multi-language support and WhatsApp help flow.

Our values

Respect

Dignity first. We speak with care and protect privacy.

Accuracy

We link to verified SA sources and review content regularly.

Action

Clear next steps over noise. Simple choices when stress is high.

FAQ

Is advice on this site professional counselling?

No. We provide general guidance and links to verified services. For emergencies or clinical support, contact professionals directly.

Can I contact your team privately?

Yes. Use the “Get Help” button or email us. We’ll connect you with appropriate services. Don’t include sensitive details in the first message.

Do you operate nationwide?

We focus on South Africa and aim to include province-specific resources where possible.
